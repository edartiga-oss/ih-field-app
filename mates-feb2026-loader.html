⚠ Four errors found in the MATES spreadsheet 1. UTL — normal vs. lognormal distribution: Spreadsheet uses Avg + K×STD (arithmetic normal). Noise Manual uses exp(ȳ + K×s) (lognormal). These are different models.
2. UCL — √14 hardcoded instead of √n: The formula =D30+(1.645*(D31/SQRT(14))) has 14 hardcoded. It should be SQRT(n). This under/overstates the UCL when n≠14.
3. UCL uses z=1.645, not t(n−1, 0.95): For small n (e.g. n=6), the t-statistic is 2.015 — using 1.645 underestimates uncertainty by ~0.6 dBA.
4. Data split by survey day, not combined by SEG: Feb 10 (n=10) and Feb 11 (n=6) are calculated separately instead of pooled as a single SEG (n=16 Vehicle Maint.).
